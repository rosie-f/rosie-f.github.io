Hi! I'm Rosie.

Current marketer. Curious designer. Professional multiple hat wearer.

Rosie is an associate marketing manager at Envoy. Previously, she was a senior rotation program analyst at Salesforce.

She has done marketing for Crayon Crunch, Designlab, and the 700-person Lean In Circles program at Salesforce, and learned design at UC Berkeley, Designlab, and General Assembly.

In her spare time, Rosie enjoys playing music, obscure board games, less obscure iOS games, hiking, food, and urban exploration. She loves to mentor young adults, as demonstrated through her time volunteering via Alpha Phi Omega, teaching 4th graders about financial literacy, and supporting her friends through quarter life crises.

Find her on LinkedIn or shoot her an email anytime.
